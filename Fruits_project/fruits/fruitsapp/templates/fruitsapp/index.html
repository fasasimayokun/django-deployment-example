FRUITS


A fruit is the mature ovary of a flowering plant, usually containing seeds.It develops from the fertilized ovary of a flower after pollination occurs. Fruits are an essential part of angiosperms (flowering plants) and play a crucial role in the reproduction and dispersal of plant species. In everyday language, "fruit" is often used to refer to sweet and edible plant products that are typically consumed as part of a meal or snack. However, in botanical terms, fruits can include a wide range of structures beyond what is commonly considered as "fruits" in culinary contexts.
